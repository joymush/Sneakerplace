About Us

Sneaker place was founded at the year 2015,with the focus originally on paps and lads. The aim of coming up with the idea was because we felt that men and lads were left out alot when it came to wear sector. So we thought of coming up with a store that will meet their neeeds from official wear,to casual wear and inner wears and that was how the idea Sneakerplace store was birthed

Over time though we got the questions of why we weren't engaged in mums and girl's clothes,and at that point we decided to be diverse but only sticked with the idea of encouraging women to engage in sporty wear.And with the help of a few advisors we decided for women and girls we would engage in unisex sporty wear and with that i mean(Sweatpants and unisex sneakers and rubber shoes),that way we got to engage both the paps,mums,lads& girls.

Sneaker place store has since grown and within the short period has taken over the fashion industry and became the most celebrated fashion line & plug in town and across the country.
